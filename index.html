About OTEC

We connect local formers...

At OTEC Concept International Limited, we bridge the gap between local farmers and global markets, specializing in the export of premium agricultural produce and raw materials. Our mission is to deliver exceptional quality products while promoting sustainability and excellence in the agricultural export industry.

OUR VISION

To provide solutions for modern and sustainable farming aimed at meeting both farmers’ and consumers’ needs, with the utmost attention for the protection of the environment and for worker safety.

OUR MISSION

To be the go to company for agricultural produce in Africa and beyond

Our business model is built on the following pillars:

  • Sustainability: We prioritize environmentally friendly practices and fair trade principles.
  • Partnership: We foster strong relationships with local farmers, producers, and international buyers.
  • Excellence: We strive for exceptional quality and service in every aspect of our operations.
  • Innovation: We continuously explore new markets, products, and technologies to stay ahead in the industry.
